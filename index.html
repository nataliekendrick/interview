Uprising graphic designer from Parsons, The New School
Natalie:

What was the moment when you knew you wanted to create digital art?

Val:

I think it was when I was seeing people create digital artwork and wanting to do the same thing as they are doing. Their graphics were so dope and fascinating I wanted to achieve that as well.

What inspired you to do digital art?

For me it is more accessible and can achieve unlimited effects, I feel like there is always something to explore and study.

What digital devices do you use to do your art?

I do both analog and digital. I do a lot of stuff with my laptop, my wacom, ipad, and camera.

What effects do you have when you create art?

I have a strong belief in typography, my favorite combo is huge texts and random images.
